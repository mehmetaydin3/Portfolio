Context & The Opportunity

The disconnect between the physical store and the digital cart.

Cole Haan needed to redefine what modern shopping meant for their brand. Their digital platform struggled with cart abandonment and friction during product discovery, while physical stores operated entirely independently from the app ecosystem.

The opportunity was clear: architect a unified omnichannel system. We needed to streamline the digital e-commerce flow and use mobile technology to fundamentally upgrade the physical retail environment.

"Can we make checking out in-store as easy as clicking 'Buy Now' online?" — Retail Strategy Lead

E-commerce Architecture

Removing the friction from discovery.

We completely overhauled the information architecture of the e-commerce platform. The previous taxonomy was confusing, causing users to bounce before finding the right style or fit.

The new architecture introduced a highly modular filtering system, allowing users to drill down by size, color, width, and collections simultaneously without page reloads.

Advanced visual filtering architecture — reducing search time by 40%

Product Design Decisions

Small interactions. Massive conversion shifts.

01 — Progressive PDPs

Comprehensive Product Experience

We redesigned the Product Detail Pages (PDP) to prioritize high-fidelity imagery alongside immediate technical specifications. By bringing user reviews, sizing guides, and technology breakdowns to the surface safely, we gave shoppers the confidence to add to cart.

Product Detail Page — prioritizing lifestyle imagery and technical specs

02 — Frictionless Checkout

Streamlined Payment Flow

We condensed the traditional multi-page checkout into a responsive, single-page accordion flow. By integrating express payment options like Apple Pay and PayPal early in the journey, we dramatically reduced the cognitive load required to finalize a purchase.

35% reduction in cart abandonment

Single-page checkout architecture with express payment integrations

Spotlight Feature

The Grandshop: A first-of-its-kind NFC retail store.

We brought the digital architecture into the physical world by designing Cole Haan's first US Grandshop concept store in Silicon Valley.

The concept was simple: Make the physical store act like an app. We embedded NFC tags across the 940-square-foot modular space. Shoppers simply tapped their phones to instantly access product info, size availability, reviews, and initiate an Apple Pay checkout.

Customers could purchase and walk out, or seamlessly ship items home — entirely bypassing traditional checkout lines. It transformed the store from a point-of-sale into an interactive physical loft.

NFC tap-to-purchase flow on iPhone — instant product access, Apple Pay checkout

Post-Purchase Excellence

A brand relationship doesn't end at checkout.

We overhauled the post-purchase experience to keep users informed and reduce support overhead. Clear order tracking interfaces and a unified FAQ hub replaced a disjointed web of transactional emails and difficult-to-search help pages.
