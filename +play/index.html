Context & Opportunity

Subscriptions were everywhere. Management was nowhere.

The average user managed 5+ streaming and digital subscriptions — each with its own app, billing cycle, and cancellation flow. There was no unified view, no plan-level awareness, and no way to discover what was already included.

Verizon saw the gap. With millions of wireless customers already receiving subscription perks through their plans, there was a clear opportunity to build a centralized marketplace that didn't just aggregate services — but made the entire subscription lifecycle frictionless.

"I'm paying for things I forgot I even subscribed to." — User research insight

The challenge wasn't just building a storefront. It was designing a system that could surface plan-specific benefits, enable 1-click activation, consolidate billing, and replace the fragmented experience users had grown to accept.

Product Vision

One marketplace. Every subscription. Plan-aware.

+play was conceived as more than a catalog. It's a plan-aware platform that understands each user's Verizon plan — surfacing relevant perks, calculating plan-specific pricing, and enabling activation without leaving the ecosystem.

The vision centered on three pillars: consolidate fragmented subscriptions, leverage plan benefits as a discovery mechanism, and reduce activation friction to a single tap.

How the system works

A user's Verizon plan determines what they see. Eligible perks surface automatically on the homepage. Subscriptions they already have can be migrated and consolidated. New activations happen through a single-screen checkout that pre-fills billing through Verizon — no credentials, no friction.

Every touchpoint in the platform — from discovery to management — is shaped by the plan relationship, turning a generic marketplace into a personalized subscription hub.

Product Design Decisions

The platform set the rules. The interface had to make them invisible.

01 — Homepage & Discovery

A marketplace that knows your plan

The homepage isn't a static catalog. It's a dynamic surface shaped by the user's Verizon plan tier. Contextual offers, trending content, and personalized recommendations replace a generic grid — surfacing what's relevant before users go looking for it.

Dynamic homepage — contextual perks and curated categories based on plan tier

02 — Subscription PDP & Activation

1-click activation, zero friction

Traditional multi-step checkout flows were replaced with a single-screen PDP that combines content preview, plan-specific pricing, and 1-click activation. Verizon handles billing automatically — no credential entry, no payment forms, no redundant steps. The subscription is live before the user leaves the page.

+52% checkout conversion

Subscription PDP — from preview to activation in a single flow

03 — Migration Flow

Migrating existing subscriptions seamlessly

Users with existing subscriptions from other platforms could migrate them into +play without losing access or paying twice. The migration flow was designed to feel like an upgrade — not a chore. Clear status indicators, inline confirmation, and automatic billing transfer made the transition invisible.

Migration experience — transfer existing subscriptions without disruption

04 — Subscription Management

A unified control center

Three-tab architecture — Subscriptions, Billing, and Offers — gives users a single hub for everything. Active subscriptions, billing history, and available upgrades live in one place. Consolidated billing through Verizon simplifies what was previously spread across 5+ separate apps and payment methods.

15M+ subscriptions managed

Unified management hub — subscriptions, billing, and offers in one place

Spotlight Feature

Discovery should feel like browsing, not searching.

The Discover tab reimagines entertainment discovery as a content-forward experience. Instead of a catalog grid, users browse through curated editorial categories, trending content, and personalized recommendations.

The experience mirrors how people actually discover entertainment — through context and mood, not keyword search. Category-based exploration, visual content cards, and contextual filtering replaced the traditional search-first pattern.

Discover experience — curated editorial categories and content-forward browsing

The Final Experience

Every touchpoint, one system.

The shipped platform spans discovery, activation, migration, and management — all unified under a single design system built for Verizon's brand and plan architecture.

From personalized homepage surfaces to 1-click checkout to consolidated billing — every interaction reinforces the same promise: your subscriptions, simplified.
